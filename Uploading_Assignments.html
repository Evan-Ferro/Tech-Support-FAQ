My course requires me to upload an assignment or document. How do I do this?

Version 1.0

• Find the form required for submission either in the “Reference Materials” section of your course or within the course content.

• Click the “Uploads” tab at the top of your course, and click the corresponding yellow “Upload” button next to the assigned upload.

• If you have issues with the uploads process, you may email Tech Support with your documents to have someone upload them for you, or to ask for assistance.
