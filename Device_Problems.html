Can I take my course on a tablet or mobile device?

Version 1.0

• Yes. Our courses are compatible on mobile devices, although some courses that contain simulators or videos may prompt you to follow a specific link for the mobile version. Please note that you cannot complete your final exam on a mobile device or tablet.

• If you have issues completing your course on a mobile device, please email Tech Support or call our office to be assisted.
